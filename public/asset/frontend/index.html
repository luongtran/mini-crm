Frequently Asked Questions

General

  • What is Ownerdesk?

    Ownerdesk is a customer supports system that lives in the cloud. It was designed to help you, the vacation rental owner, give a great customer support for your customers.

     

  • Why should I use Ownerdesk?

    What’s wrong with emails and phone calls? well, emails get lost and phone calls get forgotten, or you are just not available to take the call. Ownerdesk keeps you on top of everything.

     

  • Can I use other support software systems?

    Sure you can. There are lots of great supports systems out there, but none of them was built for you, the vacation rental owner.

     

Getting Started

  • How do I sign up to Ownerdesk?

    Signing up is easy, just go to pricing page, select the plan you want, fill in few details and you’re done. The whole process doesn’t take more than 30 seconds.

     

  • What is my own support website?

    When signing up to Ownerdesk, you get your own support website. There you will add your properties and manage your tickets. Sign up now and check it out.

     

  • How do I log in to Ownerdesk?

    Go to your subdomain (yourcompany.ownershare.com) and put your username and password.

     

  • How do customers log in to my support website?

    Customers can sign up to your support website, or even easier, login effortlessly with their google or facebook account.  

     

  • How do customers open a ticket?

    Ownerdesk was designed to be very intuitive. After logging in, customers will see a “open ticket” button, simple as that.

     
     

Pricing

  • How long is the free trial ?

    30 days.

  • What plans does Ownerdesk offer?

    You have 3 options to choose from: owner, manager and elite.

  • Which plan should I choose?

    How many properties you have. One? choose the owner plan. More than one but less than five? choose the manager plan. More than five properties? you are in the elite group.

  • How payments are made?

    Payments are made on a yearly or monthly basis, depends on your choice. paying yearly is cheaper and paying monthly gives you the flexibility to cancel anytime.

     

Other

  • Can you send me updates about Ownerdesk?

    Sure. All you need to do in order to get updates from us is to put your email address in the “Subscribe to Ownerdesk Insider” box in the page footer.
